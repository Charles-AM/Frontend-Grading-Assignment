Admission Requirements

Computer Science (CS)

  • Credit pass in English Language(C4)
  • Credit pass in Core Math(C4)
  • Credit pass in Integrated Science(C4)
  • Credit pass in Social Studies(C4)
  • Credit passes in Elective Maths(C4)
  • Credit passes in any other two electives(C4)

Information Technology (IT)

  • Credit pass in English Language(C4)
  • Credit pass in Core Math(C4)
  • Credit pass in Integrated Science(C4)
  • Credit pass in Social Studies(C4)
  • Credit passes in any other two electives(C4)
